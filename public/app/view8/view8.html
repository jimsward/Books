About Stump3

First, the name. We live in western Sonoma County amongst the giant redwoods. Taking up most of our front yard is a stump about 30 feet in diameter at the ground. We have been told that it is the third largest stump in existence; could be.

We are a small startup with the goal of producing a complete online accounting package via the MEAN stack.

At this time we have a check register with complete CRUD operations and checkbook entry lookup by date, payee, account, and amount; customer pages create and modify customer records and transactions along with creating, printing, and manipulating invoices. Profit and Loss reports, both summary and detailed are available.
